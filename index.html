Hello!

Welcome to my page. My name is Jiamin Xie (谢佳旻).

I am a second-year Ph.D. candidate working on problems in automatic speech recognition (ASR) for naturalistic data at University of Texas at Dallas , supervised by Dr. John Hansen. My research focuses on to understand and develop new methods to improve self-supervised learning features and end-to-end models for ASR, especially when there exists a training/testing domain mismatch. I finished my master's degree working on speaker diarization (the problem of "who speaks at when") for child speech data at Center for Language and Speech Processing (CLSP). Outside of lab, I enjoy playing tennis or hiking with friends and occasionally cooking (try a receipe I made a long time ago) and traveling.

Please refer to my latest CV and feel free to reach out for research discussions or fun activities.
